About the Creator

 Jackson Newman is currently a student at UC Santa Barbara pursuing an undergraduate degree in computer engineering. He prefers challenging himself in classes, among his favorites are computer science and physics. Outside of class he likes playing video games and biking on trails in Belmont. At school, he participates in the UCSB Overwatch B team and the UCSB IEEE branch. He always welcomes outside projects to improve upon coding and life skills.
